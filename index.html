TEAM FSP REQUIREMENT

The FSP requirement is a physical test spread over several exercises that a person must pass in order to join our team. It must be passed according to the different constraints: Time limits, amplitude, quality of movement execution, minimum load. To join the team he will have to pass each of the 4 movements of Street Lifting with a minimum weight according to his body weight. If he succeeds, his profile will then be analyzed to know if the motivations and objectives of the athlete correspond to those of Team FSP.


This tool allows from the estimation of your 1RM to see where you are in relation to our expectations. It is just a simulation and does not replace a real test.
